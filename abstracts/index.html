Abstract Timeline

The abstract submission will open on September 1st. The deadline for submitting the abstract is 15 October 2025. All abstracts will undergo a peer-review process conducted by the organizing and scientific committees.

Notifications of the review outcome will be sent at the end of November.

Abstract Guidelines

Please use the following LaTeX template to prepare your abstract: LaTeX Template

  • The maximum length of the abstract is 1 page including figures and references
  • Including a figure in the one page abstract is not mandatory, but it is highly recommended.

How to Submit

Abstract submission will be done through the sciencesconf system using the following link: https://ml4fluids.sciencesconf.org/.

Please register using the link above and proceed with the submission of your abstract.
